Analyzing the monthly rainfall data in the Poughkeepsie area

The dataset originating from NOAA contains the monthly rainfall for its many stations across the United States. This information dates from 1981 to 2010.

For this page, we will be focusing on Poughkeepsie, New York in a even more specific time frame. Data will include rainfall and even occurances of incomplete data.

Counting complete and incomplete monthly data

Due to many factors in climate recording, getting full, complete data is not always feasible.

Factors such as weather station maintence or absence of crew members can cause a day or few in a month to not be fully recorded.

NOAA designates these months with less than optimal information by appending an "F" after the milimeter reading for each month.

Due to the incomplete data count being easier to record and copy, we can easily include the complete/incomplete count for the full timeframe.

Chart of Complete/Incomplete Monthly Data (1981 - 2010)
